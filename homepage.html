Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions? We've got answers. If you can't find what you're looking for, feel free to contact our support team.

Is ChatterSphere free to use?

Yes, ChatterSphere offers a free plan that includes basic features for teams of up to 10 members. For larger teams or advanced features, we offer affordable Pro and Enterprise plans with additional capabilities.

How secure is my data on ChatterSphere?

ChatterSphere employs end-to-end encryption for all messages and files. Your data is encrypted both in transit and at rest. We adhere to industry-leading security standards and regularly undergo third-party security audits to ensure your information remains protected.

Can I integrate ChatterSphere with other tools?

Absolutely! ChatterSphere offers seamless integration with over 2,000 popular tools and services including Google Workspace, Microsoft 365, Slack, Trello, Asana, GitHub, and many more. Our API also allows for custom integrations if needed.

Is ChatterSphere available on mobile devices?

Yes, ChatterSphere is available on iOS and Android devices. Our mobile apps offer the same powerful features as our desktop and web versions, ensuring you stay connected on the go. You can download them from the App Store or Google Play Store.

How do I get support if I have issues?

We offer multiple support channels including an extensive knowledge base, community forums, email support, and live chat. Pro and Enterprise plans include priority support with faster response times. Our support team is available 24/7 to help resolve any issues you may encounter.
